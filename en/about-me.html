About Me

I build web applications that transform how businesses operate. Client management systems, online scheduling, process automation, email and calendar integration. Solutions that connect everything and actually work.

With over 20 years of experience in technology, I combine full-stack development with project management. I lived and worked in the United States for 20 years before returning to Argentina. I learned to code 40 years ago and stayed current: Node.js, React, Next.js, TypeScript. I completed an intensive full-stack development program (Top 10% at CoderHouse). I build applications that integrate with Google Workspace, Atlassian, and other service APIs to automate workflows.

I apply Agile methodologies and Project Management Institute principles to deliver projects that actually work. I manage teams, plan releases, and ensure every development meets business objectives.

I'm also a registered nurse. I went to nursing school to work in community health centers. This gives me a unique advantage in healthcare technology projects: I understand both the technical and clinical perspectives.

Through Pensanta.com, I help businesses with their digital presence: websites, management applications, process automation, Google Workspace migration, and custom software solutions.

If you need a web application that solves a real problem for your business, we should talk.
