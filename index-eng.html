Project and Digital Product Management

We manage projects applying PMBOK¹, SAFe² and Waterfall according to project needs. Well-executed Waterfall often delivers better results than poorly implemented Agile.

Our decisions are based on data and rigorous analysis, which tends to generate efficiency and tangible value.

We design scalable projects and products, with deliveries focused on meeting agreed Timelines and Budgets.

In large-scale Digital Products we act as external Product Owners, with technical and business perspective. We come from the world of software, delivery, product and infrastructure. We advocate for our clients' interests, speaking the language of technical and multidisciplinary teams, and ensuring results.

We use the most modern and efficient tools for management, communication, performance metrics and analytics to maintain focus, align teams and avoid deviations.

We manage complex projects with clarity, results orientation and the most fluid communication with all stakeholders.

¹ PMBOK: Project Management Body of Knowledge. Although in practice it is often called "the PMBOK book", the term refers to the body of knowledge, methods and best practices developed by the Project Management Institute (PMI), beyond its physical or digital format.

² SAFe: Scaled Agile Framework. It is a framework for applying agile principles at the organizational level, coordinating multiple teams under a shared vision, with delivery structure in programs and release trains.
